Jungle Journey

Return to the life you never knew you missed.

Swing back to a simpler time...

Do you feel out of place? Lost in a time you don't belong?
Do you often find yourself wondering what life was like before our species went global?

Well wonder no more. Our getaway promises to show you a new way of life. Or really, we should say the oldest way of life. For two weeks, immerse yourself in a thrilling jungle experience that you've only ever dared to imagine before. With no outside communication, food you can pluck straight from the trees next to your front door and the calming sounds of nature to lull you to sleep each night, no other experience will transport you like this.
